What Is NRA

The National Rifle Association of America as known as NRA is an American nonprofit organization that advocates for gun rights. The NRA has been criticized by gun control and gun rights citizens, advocacy groups, political commentators, and politicians. The organization has been the focus of intense criticism in the aftermath of high-profile shootings

Why NRA

   In the wake of the Florida school shooting, the debate over whether teachers should carry guns in class has intensified. President Donald Trump has proposed bonuses for educators who undergo gun training. State lawmakers are beginning to consider legislation. #NRA has become to a hot hashtag on twitter after Donald Trump’s announcement.

Memes

Memes are humorous images, videos, pieces of text, etc., that are copied often with slight variations and spread rapidly by Internet users. By tracing the hashtags of #NRA memes on twitter, we can see which states or areas have mentioned this hashtag more often which leads to our research question, "which area/state is more caring about the NRA or the gun control by tracing the #NRA memes’ trends on twitter.
